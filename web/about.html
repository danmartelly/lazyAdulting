About

Navigating the adult world can be hard. I've had the good fortune to have room to make mistakes because of a good education, a good job, and having my parents as a security net. But, I know not everyone has the time, energy, or resources to make the same mistakes, so the idea with this website is to give simple instructions for how to be a responsible adult.

How to use this website

Every article on this website starts with what and how to do something. The why is saved for later if you have time, because there's a lot to figure out.
